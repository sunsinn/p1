About me

I am a cataloging and metadata librarian at the Arthur and Elizabeth Schlesinger Library on the History of Women in America. I have a B.S. in Film and Photography from Ithaca College and a Masters in Library and Information Science from Simmons College.

Why I am taking this course

I audited CSCI E-10 two years ago to get a little programming under my belt and enjoyed it enough that I continued on to E-22 and E-66. As librarianship increasingy involves work with linked data, large data sets, and online services of all kinds, I thought it would be a good idea to continue my professional development in those areas.

Programming experience

The bulk of my programming experience is in Java. I have done some work with HTML, CSS, and Javascript, though nothing recently besides a Codeacademy course to brush up on the basics - I took my previous website down in 2003! Over the past year, I've focused on data structures and databases - SQL, XML in conjunction with BerkeleyDB, and NoSQL.

My work for this semester will be done on a laptop running Windows 7.
